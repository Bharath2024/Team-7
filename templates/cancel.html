Dear Valued Guest,

We understand that plans may change, and unexpected circumstances arise. To ensure transparency and fairness, we have established the following cancellation policy:

Cancellation Policy:

1. Cancellations made 72 hours prior to check-in: No cancellation fee.

2. Cancellations made within 72 hours of check-in: 50% of the booking amount will be charged.

3. No-shows and early departures: 100% of the booking amount will be charged.
